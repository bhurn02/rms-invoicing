Reading Schedule & Billing Impact

Critical: Timing Matters for Billing Accuracy

The date you perform QR meter readings directly affects the billing periods saved in the system. Understanding this timing is crucial for accurate tenant billing.

Reading Period Calculation
  • Date From: 1st day of the reading month
  • Date To: Last day of the reading month
  • Example: Reading on Sep 25 → Period: Sep 1-30
Billing Period Calculation
  • Billing From: 1st day of next month
  • Billing To: Last day of next month
  • Example: Reading on Sep 25 → Billing: Oct 1-31
Impact of Delayed Readings
Late Reading (Oct 5)

If you read meters on October 5th instead of September 25th:

  • Reading Period: Oct 1-31
  • Billing Period: Nov 1-30
  • Result: September usage billed in November
Very Late Reading (Oct 15)

If you read meters on October 15th:

  • Reading Period: Oct 1-31
  • Billing Period: Nov 1-30
  • Result: September + October usage billed together
On-Time Reading (Sep 25-29)

Recommended reading window:

  • Reading Period: Sep 1-30
  • Billing Period: Oct 1-31
  • Result: Accurate monthly billing
Best Practices for Reading Schedule
Recommended Schedule
  • Monthly: 25th-29th of each month
  • Consistency: Same date range every month
  • Planning: Schedule readings in advance
Avoid These Scenarios
  • Early Reading: Before 25th (affects previous month)
  • Late Reading: After 5th of next month
  • Irregular Schedule: Inconsistent dates
